About Me

BORN ON THE 8TH JUNE 2000, I AM A TRAINED MEDICAL ARTIST (MSC MEDICAL VISUALISATION AND HUMAN ANATOMY) WITH EXPERIENCE IN 3D MODELLING, VISUAL EFFECTS AND CADAVERIC DISSECTION.

Carrie, Medical and Visual Effects Artist

Hey there, I'm Carrie,...

... a 23 year old artist who currently finished her postgraduate degree at the Glasgow School of Art in Visualization. I am German living since 5 years in the UK where I studied and used to work as a Graffiti Artist. In my free time I love to sketch, paint, gel print and edit photos. Through my degree I found my passion to 3D art. I love photorealistic design and visual effects. Colour correction, Lighting and Glitch effects is what I live for. My style tends towards a grunge and retro design however, I can never resist the beauty of minimal and aesthetic design for long.
